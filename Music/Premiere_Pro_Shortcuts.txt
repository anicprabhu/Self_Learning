		A - All Tracks Forward

		Shift + A... All tracks BAckward

		Shift ... Only single track clip selected

		Shift + Alt ..... moves all videos on track without moving Audio / vice versa

		CTRL + Shift + V ... INsert Paste

		CTRL + V ........ OVerwrite Paste

	Ripple [B] / Roll [N]..For Transitions
		Ripple - Shorten or lengthen the clip and move other clips to fill gap
				Clips the inpoint or outpoint of a clip without changing the adjacent clips
				Used as MAtching Tool

		Roll - Affects outpoint and Inpoint of ADJACENT clips simultaneously
				USed to determine where the edit actually happens after the match


	Rate Stretch Tool [R]
		USe this to Fast forward and not much for Slow Motion

	Razor Tool [C]
		Through Edit Concept, join if they exist

		Alt + Cut... . Only cuts the audio / Video and not the linked content

		Shift + Cut .......Cuts all the clips on all Tracks

	Slip Tool [Y] and Slide Tool [U]. For Clip Contentsy
		Slip - Trims a single clips in and out point simultaneously while not affecting the adjacent clips

		Slide - Opp of Slip Tool . Does not affest the single clip in and out points but adjusts the adjacent clips

	Pen Tool [P]
		Setting Opacity / Effect Level

		Select Tool -> Ctrl Key.... used to add keyframes

	Trim Window [Shift + T]
		 GIve quick access to ripple and Roll Editing

		 Use Up and Down arrow to jump to edits and press T

		 Ctrl + Left / Right ... Move one frame

		 Ctrl + SHift + Left / Right .... Move 5 Frames
